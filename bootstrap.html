Little interview with Freddie Roach:

Interviewer: Freddie, you've had a long and storied career in boxing. What has kept you motivated to continue in the sport for so many years?

Freddie Roach: "Well, boxing is my life. I’ve always loved the sport, and even though I’m not in the ring anymore, my passion for it hasn't changed. Coaching gives me a chance to work with young fighters and help them achieve their dreams, and that’s incredibly fulfilling for me."

Interviewer: What do you think about the future of boxing? Are there any up-and-coming fighters that you’re excited about?

Freddie Roach: "There’s a lot of talent out there, and I think boxing is in a good place. I’m especially excited about some of the young fighters I’ve been working with recently. They have the skills and the drive to take the sport to new heights."

Interviewer: Finally, if you had to give one piece of advice to a young boxer just starting their career, what would it be?

Freddie Roach: "Never stop learning. Every day is an opportunity to improve. Keep working hard, stay humble, and always respect the sport."
